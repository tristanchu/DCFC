The Friends of Dartmouth Fencing was founded as a way for alumni, friends, parents, and generous individuals to support the team, whether by following our accomplishments, encouraging us as a community, or donating money to the team.

Your support is what allows us to enjoy such success as an entirely student run club team, whether it is through encouragement at our competitions or general enthusiasm and interest in what we do and what we've accomplished. We are always humbled and honored by everything you do for us; it is all thanks to you that our club has grown and expanded to what it is today.

Our Friends will receive an annual newsletter and e-mail updates throughout the season. We are grateful for the generous donations we receive that fund our equipment and travel expenses, while also helping us keep indiviudal fencer costs to a minimum. All gifts are tax deductible.

Following College guidelines, DCFC only sends out one request for donations per year. The link to donate is below; please select "Friends of Fencing" as the recipient for your gift. For other information regarding our team, please refer to this website, as we update regularly, or follow us on Facebook and Instagram. For any further inquiries, please send an email to Fencing.Club@dartmouth.edu!
