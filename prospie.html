Dear Prospective Dartmouth Fencer -

Whether you are a seasoned fencer or a beginner who has yet to step foot on the strip, let us be the first to say--welcome home. We are thrilled by your interest in DCFC, and we want to take the time here to you a little more about our team.

You are coming at an exciting time. Dartmouth Fencing is riding the wave of two successful, historic seasons. In 2014, we won silver in men's épée, gold in men's team overall, and gold in men's and women's combined team in the USACFC College Fencing National Championships. In 2015, we won gold in women's foil and gold in best women's team overall.

Here's where you come into the picture: at the core of DCFC are the people. As a completely student-run club team with no coach, it's up to all of us to coach each other, support one another, cheer everyone on, and push our teammates to the next level. We are always on the lookout for motivated people to join our team. No experience is necessary! As long as you are interested in fencing and willing to put in the time, we want YOU on our team!

What you can expect if you join DCFC:

  • Practices with drills and open fencing
  • Crazy (but safe!) road trips to competitions
  • Team dinners, pumpkin picking, cabin trips, midnight breakfasts
  • A strong community that will be here for you for the rest of your time in college (and beyond!)
Interested? Here's how you can learn more:

  • Come say hi and visit our booth in the activities fair during orientation
  • Be on the lookout for our demo and open practices during the first week of classes
  • Follow Dartmouth Fencing on social media! Facebook, Instagram, or Blitz!
  • Check this website for periodic updates

Thanks for stopping by! We hope to see you this fall, and once again, welcome home :)
-Co-Captains Tristan & Cecilia
